Paka 0.15.1 adds a Developer light mode (black on paper-white, for e-ink displays such as Light Phone 2), shows QR and Aztec codes on a compact centred stage instead of a full-width white panel, and roughly halves the APK by shipping one CPU architecture. Stable releases now attach an arm64 APK and a 32-bit APK; this is the last release line supporting Light Phone 2. Also hardens store cleanup and release verification.
