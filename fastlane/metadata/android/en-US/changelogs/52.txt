Paka 0.15.0 “Paperlight” adds in-app document capture, shared review and crop for captured or chosen photos, safer viewer-scoped photo memory, and interruption-safe encrypted restore. It fully localizes the app in English, Latvian, Estonian, Lithuanian, Finnish, Swedish, German, and Slovak, automatically following a supported device language until an in-app choice is made. Barcode fidelity, accessibility, storage recovery, camera lifecycle, and release verification are also hardened.
